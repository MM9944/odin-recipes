Shark Steak

>

Description

Shark Steak is a dish designed for alpha predators. Odin's Shark Steak recipe is an immaculate dietary staple of ultimate virility. Valhalla has truly blessed those worthy enough to consume this entree.

Ingredients

  • 3 (8 ounce) Shark Steaks: For best results use Megaldon
  • ¼ cup Soy Sauce: ONLY use Western Han dynasty fermented soy sauce
  • ¼ cup Worcestershire Sauce: This sauce must contain fermented garlic from Arthur's Camelot
  • 2 Tablespoons Onion Powder: Organic store bought
  • 2 Tablespoons Garlic Salt:
  • 2 Tablespoons Chopped Fresh Thyme:
  • 2 Tablespoons Ground Black Pepper:
  • 2 Tablespoons Chili Powder:

Steps

  1. Step 1: After harpooning the leviathan, rinse the shark steaks and trim off any skin.

  2. Step 2: Mix together the sacred ingredient,soy sauce, Worcestershire sauce, onion powder, garlic salt, thyme, black pepper, and chili powder in a small bowl to form a paste. Spread a thin layer of the soy sauce mixture on both sides of each shark steak. Place on a plate and cover. Allow to marinate in the refrigerator at least 30 minutes.

  3. Step 3: Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).

  4. Step 4: Arrange the shark on a baking sheet. Bake the shark in the preheated oven until cooked completely through, about 40 minutes, turning the steaks over once, about half-way through the cooking time.
