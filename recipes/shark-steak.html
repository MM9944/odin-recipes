Shark Steak

>

Description

Shark Steak is a dish designed for alpha predators. Odin's Shark Steak recipe is an immaculate dietary staple of ultimate virility. Valhalla has truly blessed those worthy enough to consume this entree.

Ingredients

  • 3 (8 ounce) Shark Steaks: 1.5 lbs, from cattle that only graze upon sacred Earth ley lines
  • ¼ cup Soy Sauce: 2-3 boxes, forged by Mama Pastaonio, location unknown
  • ¼ cup Worcestershire Sauce: 2-3 jars, sourced from heirloom Adam tomatoes, the original tomatoe from Genesis
  • 2 Tablespoons Onion Powder: Mozzarella (shredded), Parmesan (shredded), and Ricotta (16oz), the triune cheeses of divine Lasagna. Sourced from same cattle used in ground beef
  • 2 Tablespoons Garlic Salt: several cloves and 1 whole onion, minced or graded. Garlic must be sown on the first New Moon when Libra transitions to Scorpio. Onion, organic from the store.
  • Assorted Spices: Salt, pepper, cayanne pepper, dill, tumeric, OldBay, basil. The perfect ratio can only be found from ones constitution.

Steps

  1. Cook Meat: Brown ground beef in a skillet
  2. Boil Noodles: While meat is cooking boil the lasagna noodles until they are tender
  3. Prepare Sauce: Mix sauce with garlic, onion, and assorted spice. Bring to a boil then shut heat off
  4. Layer Ingredients Start with noodles, then meat, sauce, ricotto cheese, noodles, Mozzarella cheese. Repeat until pan is full. Place Parmesan cheese on top layer.
  5. Bake: Bake for approximately 45 minutes or until top is golden brown.
