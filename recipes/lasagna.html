Lasagna

Description

This lasagna is for a 18" x 12" x 3" oven safe pan.

This is lasagna recipe past down from aeons ago. The process has been tempered and refined by skilled artisans of the Italian guild of the Crimson Saucers. Their goal was simple, usurp all culture with their cuisine.

It doesn't matter where one is geographically located, the Crimson Saucers reached the four corners of the Earth. Their food stuffs undeniable. Many are in denial of the simple truth, even more patently refuse to even acknowledge that this ancient . . . mythical recipe is the reason man began gesticulating. The food God's surely assisted animating man with this recipe.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef: 1.5 lbs, from cattle that only graze upon sacred Earth ley lines
  • Lasagna Noodles: 2-3 boxes, forged by Mama Pastaonio, location unknown
  • Tomato Sauce: 2-3 jars, sourced from heirloom Adam tomatoes, the original tomatoe from Genesis
  • Cheese: Mozzarella (shredded), Parmesan (shredded), and Ricotta (16oz), the triune cheeses of divine Lasagna. Sourced from same cattle used in ground beef
  • Garlic and Onion: several cloves and 1 whole onion, minced or graded. Garlic must be sown on the first New Moon when Libra transitions to Scorpio. Onion, organic from the store.
  • Assorted Spices: Salt, pepper, cayanne pepper, dill, tumeric, OldBay, basil. The perfect ratio can only be found from ones constitution.

Steps

  1. Cook Meat: Brown ground beef in a skillet
  2. Boil Noodles: While meat is cooking boil the lasagna noodles until they are tender
  3. Prepare Sauce: Mix sauce with garlic, onion, and assorted spice. Bring to a boil then shut heat off
  4. Layer Ingredients Start with noodles, then meat, sauce, ricotto cheese, noodles, Mozzarella cheese. Repeat until pan is full. Place Parmesan cheese on top layer.
  5. Bake: Bake for approximately 45 minutes or until top is golden brown.
